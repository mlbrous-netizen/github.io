📌 Appendix & Master Tips
🏛️ The "Start Day" Rule (Avoiding Museum Closures) Because many major European historical sites have strict weekly closures, Day 1 of this trip must fall on a Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, or Saturday.
🔌 The Power Adapter Gauntlet You are visiting three distinct regions that use entirely different electrical outlets. Everyone in the group will need universal adapters.
📍 Essential Transit Apps Download Citymapper (London/Paris), SNCF Connect (France), and SBB Mobile (Swiss) before leaving.
🧷 Pin Cataloging on the Go Export an offline copy of your pin collection spreadsheet directly to your phone to make trading for Euro-exclusives at the Pueblo Trading Post a breeze!
🧳 Custom Luggage Tags Since your group is utilizing extensive high-speed rail networks, you'll be storing a lot of bags on shared train racks. Use your multi-material 3D printer to run off identical, brightly colored geometric luggage tags for all 10 travelers. It makes spotting your group's bags from across the train carriage incredibly easy!
